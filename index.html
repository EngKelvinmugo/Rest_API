Our Products

Maize Flour.

Our maize flour is the cornerstone of our product lineup. Meticulously milled from the finest maize grains, our maize flour boasts exceptional quality and consistency. We offer a range of maize flour options to cater to different culinary preferences and applications.

Maize Bran

Maize bran, another valuable byproduct of maize milling, is a fiber-rich ingredient that offers numerous health benefits. Our maize bran is carefully processed to retain its natural nutritional properties and high fiber content. It serves as an excellent dietary addition for individuals seeking a high-fiber diet or for incorporation into animal feed formulations.

Maize Germ.

Maize germ is a highly nutritious component extracted during the maize milling process. At Wima Millers, we take great care in preserving the inherent nutritional value of maize germ. Rich in healthy fats, vitamins, and minerals, our maize germ is a valuable ingredient for the food and feed industries.
